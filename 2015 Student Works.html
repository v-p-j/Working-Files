The module involves students to work in teams of 3-4 and create mental maps of a given area on various criteria. Students mark locations of the chosen criteria across the area and represent them on the map. The exercise is meant to facilitate easy navigation through the area. The analysis of the map reveals patterns which help building up better navigation techniques.

The exercise aims at making the students understand the nuances of interactions in everyday applications. The students learn how to make an app more intuitive, and experientially fulfilling. The exercise also involves students to learn how to make an app more user friendly and how to make is usable to the fullest. The theme for 2015 Fall semester was 'Calendars'.
